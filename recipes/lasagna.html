Lasagna

The lasagna is a tasty dish that is very hearty and meaty. The savory sauce makes for dinner anyone would love

Ingredients

  • Pasta sheets
  • 1 lb of Ground Beef
  • 1 Cup of tomato sauce
  • 2 tbs of salt
  • 3 tbs of pepper

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta sheets in boiling water
  2. Saute the Ground Beef till brown
  3. Layer the sheets, Beef, and sauce in a pan
  4. Add Salt and Pepper to taste
  5. Cook in and oven for 15 min
