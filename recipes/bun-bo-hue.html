Bun Bo Hue

This recipe is said to be a soup to rival the fomous Vietnamese dish - Pho. With many savory flavors this will blow you away.

Ingredients

  • 3 lbs of Beef Bone
  • 1 lb of Beef Shank
  • 1/2 cup of spice
  • 2 stalks of lemongrass
  • 1 onion
  • 8 oz of noodles

Steps

  1. Boil all the ingredients for 5 hours
  2. put to boil the noodles before the 5 hours is up
  3. combine into a bowl and serve
